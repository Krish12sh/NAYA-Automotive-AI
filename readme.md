NAYA is a conversational, context-aware and proactive automotive AI companion designed to explore the future of human–vehicle interaction.

Unlike traditional command-based vehicle assistants that depend on fixed phrases, NAYA uses natural-language understanding to interpret conversational, indirect and multi-step requests.

A driver can speak naturally:

> “I had a tiring day. Take me to Hyderabad Airport, set the cabin temperature to 22 degrees, and play some relaxing music.”

NAYA can understand the complete request, generate multiple actions, update the vehicle interface and respond naturally.

---

## Vision

Modern in-car assistants are often limited to predefined commands.

NAYA is designed around a broader idea:

> An automotive AI should not only execute commands. It should understand the driver, maintain journey context, reason about vehicle conditions and proactively provide useful assistance.

---

## Core Features

### Natural Conversation

NAYA can:

- Answer general questions
- Continue conversations
- Understand informal English
- Interpret imperfect sentence structures
- Use previous conversation context
- Respond naturally instead of relying only on fixed replies
